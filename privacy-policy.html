Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FinScan. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using the FinScan mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

FinScan is a personal finance management app that allows you to scan and store receipts, categorise expenses, generate financial reports, and get AI-powered insights about your spending.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information — username and e-mail address when you register.
  • Receipt data — photos you capture with the camera, amounts, dates, categories, and descriptions you enter.
  • Financial reports — PDF reports generated from your receipt data.
  • Profile photo — an optional avatar image you may upload.
  • Device & notification tokens — to deliver push notifications about completed reports (only if you enable this in Settings).
  • Location — only used at the time of scanning a receipt if you choose to attach a location to it; we do not track your location continuously.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store, organise, and display your receipts and expense categories.
  • To generate personalised financial reports in PDF format.
  • To power the AI chatbot feature (queries are sent to Google Gemini; see Section 5).
  • To send push notifications about report generation status (if enabled).
  • To send account-related e-mails (e.g. password reset).

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms. Communication between the App and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.

Receipt images and generated PDF reports are stored on our servers and are only accessible by your account.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — used for the AI chatbot and receipt OCR features. Queries sent to Gemini are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not share your full account data; only the specific message or image you submit to the chatbot is sent.
  • Brevo (SMTP) — used to send transactional e-mails. Your e-mail address is shared with Brevo solely for this purpose.

6. Permissions Used

  • Camera — to capture photos of receipts.
  • Internet — to communicate with our servers.
  • Post Notifications — to send push notifications about completed reports (Android 13+). You can revoke this at any time in your device Settings.
  • Access Fine / Coarse Location — optionally attached to receipts at scan time; never collected in the background.
  • Read / Write External Storage — on older Android versions, to save PDF reports to your device.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete individual receipts, reports, or your entire account at any time. Upon account deletion, all associated data (receipts, images, reports) is permanently removed from our servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features (e.g. notifications, location).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the e-mail address below.

9. Children's Privacy

FinScan is not directed at children under the age of 8. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Account Deletion and Data Removal

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data (receipts, images, reports, and account information) at any time.

To do so, please send an e-mail to goncalofilipecosta@outlook.pt with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the e-mail address associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
